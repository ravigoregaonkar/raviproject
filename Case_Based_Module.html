Instt. Of Medical Sciences & SUM Hospital, Bhubaneshwar

MD Dr. Bikash Kar is a Professor & Head of the Dermatology Department at the SUM Hospital, Bhubaneshwar. He completed MBBS from Veer Surendra Sai Institute of Medical Sciences and Research, Sambalpur, Odisha in 1997 and MD - Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy from Postgraduate institute of medical education and research, Chandigarh in 2001. He has over 19 years of experience as a Dermatologist. He is a member of IADVL, Association of Cosmetic Surgeons of India (ACSI). Currently provides Services/Treatments for Acne , Skin Care, Wrinkles , Skin Tag, Hair loss, and skin infections.
