About ACE Program

ACE - A Continuing Education Initiative by Abbott is a multi-year multi-therapy program that offers free access to information that has been developed from renowned Indian faculties in leading medical colleges/institutions across India to help graduate doctors in their academics through exam-oriented case-based modules, simulated videos and key resources required to upgrade clinical practices.
